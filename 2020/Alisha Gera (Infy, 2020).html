Priyansha Singh

1996, Software Engineer, Drawing Artist, Entrepreneur (at Handprints Club). Engineer by profession, artist by passion.

  1. What is the one factor that contributed most in what you have achieved in life?

    Believing in myself.

  2. What is your single biggest achievement?

    I don't have a single best achievement. Instead, I learn a new thing every single day and that is my biggest achievement.

  3. What is the one lesson that you learnt the hard way?

    I have learnt that patience and hardwork are the 2 most important things in life.

  4. What is the one thing that you would like to change about India to make it a better country?

    Education system. Everything is based on what we learn, our thoughts, our mindset, and if change that everything will change for good.

  5. What would be your message to anyone aspiring success?

    Just work hard and give your 100% but never ever strive for success.

  6. What is the biggest mistake that you have ever made?

    Thinking that success = happiness, but it's not so. I am happy that I know it today!

  7. Please tell us about the toughest times you have ever had, the most testing times that you have been through.

    When I was appearing for engineering entrance exams!

  8. What is the one thing that motivates you the most?

    Me, myself! I am a self motivated person. Whenever I feel low I go in front of mirror, remind myself of how wonderful I am.

  9. What is the one thing that worries you the most?

    Losing loved ones.

  10. Please tell us about your five-year plan, personally and professionally.

    Just living each day as the last day of my life! I don't think this should ever be a question. Instead we should motivate each one to live for today and not for future, as future is just an illusion.
