Jayeta Sharma

1993, CRM Consultant

  1. What is the one factor that contributed most in what you have achieved in life?

    My mother - My biggest support

  2. What is your single biggest achievement?

    There is no big as such. Small little good things.

  3. What is the one lesson that you learnt the hard way?

    That everything is temporary.

  4. What is the one thing that you would like to change about India to make it a better country?

    Push to not see women as objects. Dubai was totally different.

  5. What would be your message to anyone aspiring success?

    To never give up. The last time and one more time after that when you will try, you will succeed.

  6. What is the biggest mistake that you have ever made?

    There is no biggest, I have learnt something from all of my mistakes.

  7. Please tell us about the toughest times you have ever had, the most testing times that you have been through.

    When I was at bedrest for 2 months. Suffering from health issue. It was horrible.

  8. What is the one thing that motivates you the most?

    When I fail. Seriously when I fail at something, the next is motivation which comes automatically. Else YouTube videos and someone uplifting through his/her words (external motivation)

  9. What is the one thing that worries you the most?

    When people don’t understand me. Or do not even try to understand. When people don’t try to understand why I am saying certain things.
