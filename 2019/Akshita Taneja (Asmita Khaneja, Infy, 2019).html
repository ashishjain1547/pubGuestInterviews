  4. What is the one thing that you would like to change about India to make it a better country?

IQ, so they know how to overcome the challenges and achieve the best in world and EQ, so that they come back to their roots and make their own people benefit. This would definitely require both high IQ and EQ to face the odds!

  5. What would be your message to anyone aspiring success?

    Persistence is key to success. So, never say never!

  6. What is the biggest mistake that you have ever made?

    Doubting myself for words of others!

  7. Please tell us about the toughest times you have ever had, the most testing times that you have been through.

    Loosing someone close to heart in the hands of death!

  8. What is the one thing that motivates you the most?

    My loved ones.
